Our Story

A Passion for Baking

Sweet Bliss Bakery began in 2018 in Emma's tiny apartment kitchen, where her love for creating delightful treats for friends and family blossomed into something more.

What started as weekend passion projects quickly gained popularity through word of mouth, and soon Emma was receiving orders for birthday celebrations, office gatherings, and special events.

Today, our home bakery continues to create handcrafted delicacies with the same attention to detail and love as those first batches. We use only the finest ingredients, sourced locally whenever possible, to ensure every bite brings joy and satisfaction.
